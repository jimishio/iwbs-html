Know About IWBS

Our Vision

The IWBS motto is - aa-yogya-ha puru-sho na-asti – meaning, there is no person who is not able. At IWBS, the belief is that we all have what it takes to be the best in our chosen pursuit. Preparation and perseverance are the two things that create the difference. We believe everyone can learn wines, spirits, brews, and ferments, and at any age. Based on this philosophy, we at IWBS hope to never create yet another wine server or bartender. We wish to merely help people evolve an understanding of their own senses, opening them up as budding enthusiasts to the world of beverages.


To ensure that only the best and the most authentic knowledge is delivered, IWBS brings the information straight from its source. Our team has associations with the international wine schools, academies, wine governing bodies, appellation heads, winemakers, distillers, brewers, and craft producers. All information provided is certified and is delivered with utmost confidence of being the most recent and relevant.

Why be a Beverage Professional?

Anyone who enjoys their drink will tell you that the enjoyment is heightened when there is a deeper understanding of and appreciation for what we hold in our glasses. The world of wines and beverages is growing fast but there is a paucity of educated professionals. The consumer today is well-travelled and discerning and expects a high level of service that is at par with global standards.. Hence the need for young professionals who don't just crunch numbers but feel passionately about their products. It isn’t simply enough to know cocktails and wines; a professional server must be be capable of engaging in and sustaining conversations around beverages of all manners with their clients. They should know how to pour and, more importantly, when to stop., The courses at IWBS are geared towards helping your grow in all aspects of service to help transform you into a fine astute professional.


If you wish to become a beverage expert with charisma, knowledge, a fine palate and with excellent guest management skills in the field, this is a brilliant opportunity in the industry. And to earn you that knowledge and skills, IWBS is your quintessential launch pad.
