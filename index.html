Institute of Wine + Beverage Studies

The Institute of Wine + Beverage Studies (IWBS) is India’s first internationally-recognised wine and beverage training facility and the largest dedicated training institute of its kind. It aims at providing the best education and qualifications by bringing together the global standards of academia, international trends, and skills, and delivering them in an Indian context. Be it an globally -recognised professional, or a well-rounded enthusiasts, an all comprehensive beverage learning encompasses a good balance of practical and theoretical academic delivery. IWBS offers India’s first-of-its-kind courses developed by Indian-international team of sommeliers and beverage professionals that aims at creating professionals of global quality.


It welcomes students, from those looking to carve out a successful career in the burgeoning beverage industry for themselves, to industry professionals who wish to enhance their career prospects by acquiring deeper knowledge and skills get certified at the highest level . The courses have been designed keeping in mind the rigours and demands of the industry. Once a graduate, one can confidently ascend to the top position in the hierarchy of any hospitality operation.


IWBS also helps the curious novice who wishes to simply learn how to appreciate and enjoy the stuff in their glasses better as also to make more informed choices while purchasing at a shop, bar or restaurant.


Our mentors and educators will help you lay the foundations for an unparalleled beverage experience in a technical, structured, and yet non-intimidating manner.

Why be a Beverage Professional?

Anyone who enjoys their drink will tell you that the enjoyment is heightened when there is a deeper understanding of and appreciation for what we hold in our glasses. The world of wines and beverages is growing fast but there is a paucity of educated professionals. The consumer today is well-travelled and discerning and expects a high level of service that is at par with global standards.. Hence the need for young professionals who don't just crunch numbers but feel passionately about their products. It isn’t simply enough to know cocktails and wines; a professional server must be be capable of engaging in and sustaining conversations around beverages of all manners with their clients. They should know how to pour and, more importantly, when to stop., The courses at IWBS are geared towards helping your grow in all aspects of service to help transform you into a fine astute professional.


If you wish to become a beverage expert with charisma, knowledge, a fine palate and with excellent guest management skills in the field, this is a brilliant opportunity in the industry. And to earn you that knowledge and skills, IWBS is your quintessential launch pad.
