Certificate 1 in Wines

An introductory qualification, it is the perfect start for someone who wishes to delve into wines. This two-day program offers the basic introduction to main wine styles and gyaan on its various aspects. Best suited for hotel management students,new entrants in the liquor trade,front-line professionals looking at strengthening their wine fundamentals and wine enthusiasts who wish to go a step further than simply drinking the beverage.


At this level, our educators will guide you with individual attention, and aid you in exploiting your senses to explore a wine. You will be introduced to the basics of wines and primary terms used to describe it, key varietals, the skills of opening, serving, tasting wines, and commenting on them, all intrinsic to the art of sommellerie.


For enthusiasts, this level is a great way to get acquainted with wines and to never feel intimidated again when faced with a wine list, or asked to try and comment on a wine that someone has just offered you.

As a part of the course you will learn:

  • In-depth study of international varietals and wine styles
  • Introduction to key wine producing countries and regions, their characteristics and their wines
  • The science of nature and climate, and its play on the wine style
  • Other factors affecting a wine’s style and price
  • Common labelling terms on a bottle indicating its quality levels
  • Introduction to fortified, sweet, and sparkling wines
  • Introduction to spirits, production processes, quality terms, and influencing factors
  • Structured tasting and developing tasting notes
  • Service, pronunciation, and guest handling
  • Food and wine matching, influences, factors influencing a perfect and a daring pairing
  • Guidelines on professional service protocols, cellar management, and health and safety
Assessment

The Certificate 2 in Wines + Beverages is assessed by means of a 50 multiple-choice questions' exam to be completed in 60 minutes. Candidates are required to get a minimum of 35 correct answers to graduate the program.


All examination papers are developed and marked by the IWBS. Upon successful completion for the course candidates will receive their results via email only. Successful candidates will be requested for their postal address where passing certificates and a lapel pin will be posted within 2-4 weeks of the examination date. The same can be collected from our office as well.

Cancellation Policy

In order to cancel your booking write to us at mail@iwbs.in along with a cancellation and refund request. Should the request reach us 15 days prior to the course's starting date, no cancellation charge will be levied. However, a cancellation charge of INR15000 will be levied, in case the the request is made less than 15 days before the course starting date. No refunds will be made in case the request is received 7 days before the beginning of the program. In case of cancellation of the course by IWBS full fee will be refunded.

Note: Applicant must be above the legal drinking age in their city.
